Some sample populations

For use as gender and racial diversity references

This is a small side-project to produce some sample populations in order to have a point of comparison when discussing the degree of diversity in UK technology companies. I am doing this by producing sample populations based on the population at large and other similar companies. I also have plans to extend this to be able to produce populations representing the weighted average of graduate recruitment.

Currently, this takes the form of a spreadsheet.
